Discover The Beauty Of Iran

Rugs have a history of thousands of years in various regions of the world. In a way, rugs are the visual language of cultural changes throughout the years and centuries.

We, as a three generation family have been in rug business from early 1900's, provide the most genuine, rich quality rugs around the world and all related rug care and services to our local community and surrounding cities in Berlin, Oslo, Amsterdam.

We have earned our reputation through pure and honest customer service and never compromise the quality of our products for other business purposes.

All the prices on website have carefully been assigned based on the real value of rugs and their current conditions. If you really like a specific rug but need to evaluate your budget or make some payment arrangement please do not hesitate to call us. We will try our best to match your needs.
